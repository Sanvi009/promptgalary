The Prompt 

Create a hyper-realistic digital artwork of a baby [Animal name] sitting comfortably on a vibrant peach fluffy bean bag, floating peacefully above a sea of clouds. The [Animal name] wears modern white over-ear headphones and uses a sleek silver Apple MacBook placed naturally on its lap. Next to the [Animal name] is a detailed, photo-realistic Starbucks coffee cup. The background is a dreamy sky filled with soft clouds, radiant sun rays breaking through during golden hour, and delicate white feathers gently drifting down from above. Emphasize natural animal anatomy, realistic fur texture, lifelike lighting and shadows, and ultra-high-resolution details. Style should combine cinematic lighting, magical realism, and surreal yet believable visual storytelling.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, deploy it for animal-themed ads, coffee branding, or surreal art in cafes and tech. Preserve "hyper-realistic digital artwork," "baby [Animal name]," "vibrant peach fluffy bean bag," and "Starbucks coffee cup" for magical realism. Do not modify the headphones, MacBook, or golden hour. Changing animal to "kitten" could produce cute tech visuals, leading to fur textures, while panda keeps dreamy, lifelike results.
