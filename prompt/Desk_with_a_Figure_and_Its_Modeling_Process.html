How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, employ it for intelligent figurines, desk decor, or 3D showcases in tech and hobbies. Preserve "1 / 7 scale intelligent figurine," "real environment," "round transparent acrylic base," and "Zbrush modeling process" for realism. Do not change the no-text base or BANDAI packaging. Optionally vary desk items; for example, adding mouse could yield setup visuals, leading to illusions, while original keeps detailed, packaged outcomes.
