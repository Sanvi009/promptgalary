How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, incorporate it into boutique signage, luxury fashion, or 3D modeling for retail and architecture. Preserve "luxurious 3D metallic hanging," "'ZARA' in bold, elegant font," "polished reflective gold," and "modern luxury fashion boutique" for sophistication. Do not modify the warm ambient lighting or ceiling details. Optionally vary font slightly; for example, more cursive could yield elegant visuals, resulting in reflections, while bold keeps premium, architectural outcomes.
