The Prompt 

[Regal Threads] [Noir Velvet Tuxedo Blazer] crafted from [premium black velvet] with [sleek satin shawl lapels] and [tailored slim-fit silhouette] that radiates refined luxury. Displayed against a [deep wine-red gradient background] to evoke a rich, opulent atmosphere, illuminated with [moody directional spotlight] that enhances the depth of velvet texture and lapel sheen. Positioned on a [polished dark walnut surface] for a warm yet upscale contrast.Captured from a [45-degree front angle] to highlight the elegant curve of the lapels and the precise tailoring of the blazer's cut, with [shallow depth of field] ensuring [sharp subject focus] while softly blurring the rich background. The mood is [intense], [luxurious], and [high-fashion editorial], perfect for a [formalwear luxury ad campaign] and [premium menswear e-commerce display].The [style] is [luxury studio fashion photography] with an emphasis on texture, contrast, and timeless elegance.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, employ it for fashion e-commerce, product photography, or luxury ads in menswear and editorial campaigns. Apply it by replacing "[Regal Threads]," "[Noir Velvet Tuxedo Blazer]," etc., with brand specifics, preserving "crafted from [premium black velvet]," "deep wine-red gradient background," "45-degree front angle," and "luxury studio fashion photography" for elegant mood. Do not change the shallow depth of field or texture emphasis. Changing materials to "silk" could yield smoother visuals, resulting in opulent shots, while velvet keeps rich, high-fashion outcomes.
