Luxury Beverage Can Ad

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2025-09-20
Author: promptgalary

Generated Images

The Prompt 

A luxurious 8K food photography image in 3:4 aspect ratio. A can of [beverage brand] is styled for a high-end restaurant photo shoot. The can sits on a polished dark wooden table with soft ambient lighting. Behind it, a blurred fine-dining setting with elegant tableware, a folded white linen napkin, and subtle gold accents. Studio lighting highlights the glossy texture of the can, with condensation droplets for a cold, refreshing effect. Shallow depth of field, cinematic focus, and a warm, sophisticated tone evoke luxury and nostalgia. Perfect for a high-end beverage campaign.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it in high-end beverage campaigns, restaurant menus, or luxury product shoots for nostalgic and sophisticated branding. Use AI tools by replacing "[beverage brand]" with "Coca-Cola" or "Pepsi," keeping "luxurious 8K food photography," "3:4 aspect ratio," "polished dark wooden table," and "shallow depth of field" intact for cinematic focus. Do not modify the condensation droplets or warm tone. For instance, changing to "whiskey brand" could produce elegant bar visuals, resulting in gold-accented shots, whereas "soda" might create casual yet premium displays with nostalgic, inviting effects.
