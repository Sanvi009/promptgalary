The Prompt 

"Create an ultra-realistic (hyper-realistic) dramatic scene, frozen in time, of the person from the attached image, running ahead on a street in central Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. He has a shocked and surprised facial expression with wide-open eyes and an open mouth. The man is very muscular and has a well-trained physique, he is wearing a short-sleeved Hawaiian shirt (vibrant white/orange palm print) and
shorts in the same pattern, billowing violently from the fast wind from running. Behind him, an Airbus A320 from Virgin Atlantic is about to crash into the ground with the nose pointing diagonally downwards. Both of the aircraft engines is burning heavily and strong flames are shooting out from it. In the background, the Petrona Twin Towers are visible, where one of the towers is burning heavily and the middle walkway between the towers is about to collapse to the ground. Several skyscrapers
along the path where the man is running are burning on different floors and there is a lot of debris flying to the ground, - capture the moment when a helicopter dramatically crashes into a skyscraper and explodes dramatically, a big massive fireball and smoke plume is created that illuminates the stage with orange and red light. All the mighty skyscrapers and the magical view - frame the scene, the view reflects both the chaos and the explosion. Captured at midday with razor-sharp shadows
enhanced by the dramatic light from the explosion. The composition focuses on the dramatic moment of the collision with dynamic movement and debris. The image is taken from a high bird's eye view, wide angle, documentary photography style, phase one 1Q4 detail, f/8 aperture, high bird's eye view, wide angle lens, increase saturation 12%, increase contrast 5%, increase sharpness/clarity 7%, format widescreen 16:9."

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, deploy it for disaster simulations, action movie posters, or aviation safety visuals in film and news. Preserve "ultra-realistic (hyper-realistic) dramatic scene," "running ahead on a street in central Kuala Lumpur," "Airbus A320 crashing," and "16:9" for chaos. Do not change the midday shadows or bird's eye view. Optionally vary airline; for example, "Delta" could yield different flames, resulting in debris, while Virgin keeps explosive, saturated outcomes.
