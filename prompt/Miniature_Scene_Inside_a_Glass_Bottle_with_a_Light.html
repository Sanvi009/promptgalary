The Prompt 

A miniature scene inside a transparent glass bottle with a cork lid. At the top of the bottle, there's a small light bulb emitting a glow. Beneath the light, a miniature figure of a cute girl is seated on a pile of rocks. The person appears to be having selfie and drinking Starbucks. The bottle is being held by a hand, suggesting it's a handheld art piece or decorative item.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, employ it for miniature art, decorative items, or whimsical photography in crafts and gifts. Preserve "miniature scene inside a transparent glass bottle," "tiny light bulb," "miniature figure of a cute girl," and "handheld art piece" for surprise. Do not change the cork lid or selfie pose. Optionally vary activity; for example, "reading book" could yield studious visuals, resulting in detailed rocks, while Starbucks keeps casual, creative outcomes.
