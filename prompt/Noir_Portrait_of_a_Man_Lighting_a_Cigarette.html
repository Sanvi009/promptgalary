To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for cinematic portraits, lifestyle photography, or noir-themed ads in fashion and film. Preserve "cinematic low-light portrait," "man sitting relaxed on a couch," "open button silk shirt," and "moody, dramatic atmosphere" for cool attitude. Do not change the cigarette lighting or dark background. Optionally vary drink; for example, wine could yield elegant visuals, leading to shadows, while whiskey keeps calm, detailed outcomes.
