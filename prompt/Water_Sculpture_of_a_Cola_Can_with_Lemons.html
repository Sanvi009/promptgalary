Water Sculpture RC Cola Can

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2025-09-20
Author: promptgalary

Generated Images

The Prompt 

"Create a high-resolution, realistic studio shot of a [RC COLA] beverage can formed entirely from water, emerging from a reflective water pool on the floor. The can should maintain the shape and texture of a real [RC] can, infused with glowing accents in [orange, yellow, and white] colors. The background features a smooth gradient of these same colors, enhancing the vibrant, refreshing atmosphere. Studio lighting shouldilluminate the scene without visible light sources, giving it a polished, modern advertising aesthetic. Place one whole lemon and one cut lemon beside the can, accurately scaled. The camera should capture the scene from the front in a 9:16 aspect ratio.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for beverage advertising, product photography, or e-commerce listings in soda and refreshment brands. Input it into generators by substituting "[RC COLA]" and "[RC]" with brand names like "Pepsi" or "Coke," and colors "[orange, yellow, and white]" accordingly, preserving "formed entirely from water," "emerging from a reflective water pool," "9:16 aspect ratio," and "studio lighting" for modern aesthetic. Do not change the lemons or gradient background. Changing colors to "blue, white, red" could result in patriotic drink visuals, while keeping original might yield refreshing citrus ads with polished, vibrant outcomes.
