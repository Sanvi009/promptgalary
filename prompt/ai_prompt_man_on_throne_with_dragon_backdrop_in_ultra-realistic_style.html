Ultra-Realistic AI Art Prompt: Powerful Man on Throne with Dragon Backdrop

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2025-09-20
Author: promptgalary

Create an ultra-realistic 8K AI image of an Asian man sitting on a golden throne. He wears a sharp black suit with a red tie, holding a lit cigar with smoke rising. Around him stand six women in black dresses embroidered with red dragons, posing with confidence. The scene takes place in a dark Japanese-style room, lit with golden lights and a giant dragon painting on the wall. This AI prompt captures power, authority, and cinematic drama.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic photos of 8K quality, 9:16 ratio. Using [picture reference], keep the original face, hair, shape, and expression exactly like the reference. An Asian man sat on a gilded classical style throne, wearing an elegant black suit with a black shirt and a blood red tie. His face was cold and full of power. The left foot rests on the right thigh, the right hand holds a large cigar that is lit with thick smoke rising. Six beautiful women dressed in tight black dresses embroidered with red dragons with open cleavage, posed seductively around her. The backdrop of a modern Japanese-style room is dark with dim gold pendant lights, black wood ornaments, and a large painting of a golden dragon behind it. High contrast dramatic light, emphasizing the atmosphere of a dark world that is majestic, dangerous, and full of authority.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload your reference photo so the AI keeps the original face and expression. Keep the throne, suit, cigar, and red tie since these give the image its powerful look. You can change details like the background, number of women, or clothing designs to make it fit your own style, but keep the lighting dramatic for the best result.

Frequently Asked Questions
