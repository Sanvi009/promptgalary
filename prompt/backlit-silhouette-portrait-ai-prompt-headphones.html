Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a backlit silhouette portrait?
    A backlit silhouette portrait places the light source directly behind the subject. This creates a bright background while the subject becomes dark or partially dark, forming an outline or silhouette. It emphasizes shape and form over facial details.
  • What do the Midjourney parameters mean?
    --ar 9:16 sets the aspect ratio. --raw applies the RAW image mode for less processing. --profile applies specific style profiles (1mjkpn5 fvd6fe6 are custom profile codes). --stylize 500 controls how strongly the AI applies its artistic interpretation (range 0-1000).
  • Will my face be visible in the silhouette?
