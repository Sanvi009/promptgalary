Blue Hour Side Profile: Close-Up Portrait with Eyes Closed

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-13
Author: promptgalary

Create a cinematic blue hour portrait featuring a close-up side profile with eyes closed. This prompt preserves exact facial identity from an uploaded image, combining cool deep blue lighting with subtle warm highlights on natural skin texture and freckles.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

cinematic blue hour portrait, close-up side profile of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image , eyes closed, calm introspective expression, cool deep blue lighting dominating the scene, subtle warm key light grazing the face, visible freckles and natural skin texture, soft specular highlights, minimal clean blue background, shallow depth of field, gentle falloff, realistic photography. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear reference photo showing your face, especially side profile details and freckles. 2. Use this prompt in AI image generators like Midjourney or Stable Diffusion. 3. Paste the entire prompt including the 9:16 aspect ratio. 4. Generate multiple versions to get the best balance of cool blue and warm accent lighting. 5. If freckles aren't visible enough, add 'prominent freckles' or 'detailed skin texture' to variations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is blue hour photography?
    Blue hour is the period after sunset or before sunrise when the sky takes on a deep blue tone. It creates soft, cool, diffused light perfect for moody portraits.
  • How do I get both blue lighting and warm key light in one image?
    The prompt balances both: cool blue light dominates the scene, while a separate warm light source gently hits the face. This mimics real lighting setups with mixed color temperatures.
  • What if I don't have freckles in real life?
    Remove 'visible freckles' from the prompt, or replace with other skin details like 'visible pores' or 'natural skin imperfections' for realism.
