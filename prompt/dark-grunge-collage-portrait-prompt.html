Dark Grunge Collage Portrait with Pegasus and Police Car AI Prompt

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-12
Author: promptgalary

Generate an artistic portrait where you stand against a black-and-gray collage featuring a police car, flying Pegasus, perfume bottle, newspaper text, and record label. Hand on face, loose black shirt, light beige pants, soft white outline glow, 9:16.

The Prompt 

use my photo same to same from the uploaded image posing with one hand on my face , wearing a loose black shirt and light beige pants. i standing against an artistic black-and-gray collage background. The collage includes elements like a police car, flying (Pegasus), perfume bottle, newspaper text, record label, and abstract paint textures. Aesthetic dark grunge mood with soft white outline glow around the person, realistic lightings ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your portrait photo as a reference image. 2. Copy the full prompt exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use a high image weight setting (--iw 2 in Midjourney) to preserve your facial features and clothing. 6. Generate multiple times until collage elements appear clearly.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will the AI keep my exact face from the photo?
    Yes, if you use a strong image weight (--iw 1.5–2 in Midjourney) or reference strength (0.8–1 in DALL-E). The prompt explicitly says 'same to same from the uploaded image'.
  • What does 'soft white outline glow' look like?
    A thin, subtle halo of white light tracing the edges of your body. It separates the subject from the collage background and adds a surreal, editorial effect.
  • Can I change the collage items to my own ideas?
    Absolutely. The prompt structure works with any list of objects. Just replace the five items while keeping the 'black-and-gray collage' style.
