How to Create a Newspaper Room Editorial Portrait

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-03-06
Author: promptgalary

This prompt generates a modern, high-fashion editorial portrait set inside a unique room fully covered in black-and-white newspaper pages. Featuring soft diffused lighting and a contemporary aesthetic, it is perfect for fashion bloggers, digital artists, and photographers looking to create striking, magazine-quality imagery with a strong typographic backdrop.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Modern editorial portrait of a confident [man/woman/person] in [outfit], posed [pose], with [accessory]. Inside a box room fully covered in black-and-white newspaper pages. Soft diffused studio lighting, clean off-center composition, contemporary fashion style, ultra-high resolution.

How to Use This Prompt

Step 1: Replace [man/woman/person] with your specific subject. Step 2: Describe the [outfit], such as 'a tailored crimson suit' or 'an oversized avant-garde trench coat'. Step 3: Define the [pose], like 'leaning casually against the wall' or 'looking intensely over the shoulder'. Step 4: Add a compelling [accessory], such as 'vintage aviator sunglasses' or 'a leather briefcase'. Step 5: Modify the lighting from 'Soft diffused studio lighting' to 'harsh cinematic spotlight' if you want a moodier, high-contrast effect.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I change the language or style of the newspaper in the background?
    You can easily customize the background by adding specific descriptors to the prompt. Instead of 'black-and-white newspaper pages', try using phrases like 'vintage French newspaper pages', 'Japanese kanji newspaper clippings', or 'financial stock market newspapers'.
  • Can I change the lighting to make the image look more dramatic?
    Yes. The prompt currently uses 'Soft diffused studio lighting' for an even, flattering look. To make it more dramatic, change this to 'harsh cinematic spotlight', 'gritty direct on-camera flash', or 'moody low-key lighting'.
  • What makes this prompt look like a professional magazine shot?
    The editorial feel is achieved by combining 'clean off-center composition' and 'contemporary fashion style' with 'ultra-high resolution'. This mimics the intentional framing, styling, and clarity found in professional high-end fashion photography.
