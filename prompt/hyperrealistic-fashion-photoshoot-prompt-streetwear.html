Hyperrealistic AI Fashion Photoshoot Prompt for Streetwear

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-01-30
Author: promptgalary

An AI prompt to create realistic cinematic fashion photos of a person in bold, dark chocolate streetwear, seated casually on a white bean bag against a minimal grey background.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Hyper-realistic cinematic fashion photoshoot of me same to same from uploaded image . i has the same physique. Seated casually on a deep white bean bag, i wears bold all-dark chocolate oversized streetwear: a stylish trench coat over a clean white inner t-shirt, wide white pants, chunky sneakers, and a silver watch. The background is minimal with an aesthetic grey tone.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Copy the prompt text. 2. Paste it into your AI image generator (like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion). 3. For best results, use an image of yourself as a reference if the tool allows. 4. Generate the image. You can tweak words like 'dark chocolate' or 'grey tone' to change the colors.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What AI image generator is best for this fashion prompt?
    This prompt works well in Midjourney for realism, DALL-E 3 for accurate detail, and Stable Diffusion with a photorealistic model. Use the one you have access to.
  • How do I get the AI to use my own face in the photo?
    Use the 'image prompt' or 'reference' feature in your AI tool. Upload a clear photo of yourself and include it alongside the text prompt. The phrase 'same to same from uploaded image' in the prompt hints at this.
  • Can I use this prompt for female fashion photos?
    Yes. The prompt is adaptable. You can change 'me' to 'a woman' or simply use it as-is; many AI generators will produce a neutral or interpretative result based on your reference image.
