Manga Wall Cinematic Fashion Portrait Prompt

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-17
Author: promptgalary

Generate a cinematic fashion portrait with your exact likeness standing against a wall covered in black-and-white manga panels. Perfect for luxury editorial photos with a pop culture edge.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

A cinematic fashion portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded photo standing against a wall fully covered with black-and-white manga comic panels. Face must be 100% identical to the uploaded reference image — exact same facial features, bone structure, jawline, eyes, nose, lips, skin tone, hairstyle, and overall identity. Do not alter or reinterpret the face. Preserve likeness perfectly. i wears a tailored beige suit jacket over a deep navy blue dress shirt, paired with a green tie featuring subtle yellow polka dots and a matching pocket square. i adjusting my tie with one hand, showcasing a luxury black wristwatch. Round black sunglasses, mysterious composed expression, neatly slicked-back hair. Dramatic studio lighting with soft shadows and high contrast, ultra-detailed skin texture, sharp focus, editorial fashion photography style, symmetrical centered composition, 9:16 aspect ratio, photorealistic, luxury aesthetic, cinematic color grading. do according to my gender Camera: Medium close-up Lens: 85mm portrait lens Aperture: f/2.0 Lighting: Soft key light + subtle rim light 8K resolution, ultra realistic, high detail

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, front-facing photo of the person you want to feature. 2. Copy the full prompt and paste it into your AI image generator (like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion). 3. For best results, use the same aspect ratio (9:16) and include the camera settings if your tool allows parameters. 4. Run the generator. If the face isn't a perfect match on the first try, adjust the reference image weight or run a few variations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this prompt to make a portrait of someone else?
    Yes, just upload their clear photo as the reference image instead of your own. The prompt is designed to preserve the likeness of whoever is in the uploaded image.
  • Which AI tool works best for this prompt?
    Midjourney and Stable Diffusion are excellent choices because they handle both specific camera settings and character consistency well. DALL-E 3 can also produce great results, especially with the detailed description.
  • What does 'do according to my gender' mean?
    This instruction tells the AI to apply the clothing description (suit, tie) in a way that fits the gender of the person in the uploaded photo, ensuring the final image looks natural.
