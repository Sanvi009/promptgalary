The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic DSLR camera realism portrait of me with exact same facial and physical features captured via mirror selfie, vertical frame, mid-torso to upper-thigh crop, camera positioned at chest height facing the mirror, straight-on perspective with a subtle downward visual axis created by the subject’s lowered gaze. Facial expression serious, calm, introspective; lips closed and neutral, jaw relaxed. Eyes directed downward, partially concealed beneath the shadow of a wide-brim fedora hat; eyes remain the visual focal point despite shadow. Well-groomed beard and moustache, defined facial structure and nose bridge. exact same skin tone with natural warm undertones rendered realistically, visible pores, fine skin grain, micro-textures, subtle imperfections, true-to-life highlights and shadow transitions, absolutely no skin smoothing, no beauty or AI filters. Clothing: dark knitted sweater with bold, high-contrast white abstract pattern across chest and sleeves; dark jeans; fedora hat with a small metallic side accent. Environment: dimly lit modern bathroom, mirror showing faint water spots and smudges, tiled grid wall softly visible in background; sink counter and curved faucet present in the lower foreground, slightly blurred for depth realism. Lighting: single overhead bathroom light slightly forward-facing, soft yet directional, producing strong shadow under the hat brim and chin, gradual tonal falloff across the torso; deep blacks, controlled highlights, high contrast monochrome DSLR look. Shallow depth of field with natural optical blur, background separation intact. Captured on a professional full-frame DSLR camera, 85mm prime lens realism, wide aperture look, sharp natural focus on facial features and eyes, true optical depth, accurate proportions, film-grade contrast, HDR tonal range, cinematic realism, authentic photographic texture, non-AI, non-stylized, true camera-captured look.aspect ratio 9 : 16

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the skin to look natural without AI smoothing?
    The prompt includes 'absolutely no skin smoothing, no beauty or AI filters' and 'visible pores, fine skin grain' which tells the AI to preserve natural texture.
  • Can I change the hat style?
    Yes, replace 'wide-brim fedora hat' with 'baseball cap', 'beanie', or remove hat entirely for different looks.
  • Why specify 85mm prime lens?
    85mm is the classic portrait lens focal length that creates flattering facial proportions and natural perspective that AI recognizes.
