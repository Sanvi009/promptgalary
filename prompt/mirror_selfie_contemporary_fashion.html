Location: minimalist indoor setting with concrete floor, grey walls, visible pillar or structural element. Lighting: soft, even overhead light. Mood: casual, contemporary, stylish, confident, urban. Color palette: dark grey, light blue, tan, natural skin tones, accents of golden yellow and silver. Style: realistic, high resolution, sharp focus, 8K, natural skin texture. Negative prompt: blurry, low quality, cartoon, anime, watermark, text, extra limbs, deformed, bad anatomy, bad hands, fake, distorted, oversaturated, unrealistic, grainy.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload the reference photo for face and hair accuracy. Copy the prompt into your AI generator, keeping the 8K resolution and natural lighting settings. For Midjourney, use `--ar 2:3 --v 5 --q 2` for full-body vertical framing. For Stable Diffusion, enable face restoration and high-detail rendering. Focus on maintaining realistic textures, natural skin, and sharp details on clothing, shoes, and background elements. Apply negative prompts to avoid blurriness, extra limbs, or unrealistic distortions.
