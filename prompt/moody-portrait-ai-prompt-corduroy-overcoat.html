Moody Portrait AI Prompt with Corduroy Shirt and Overcoat

Author: promptgalary

Generate a cinematic, high-angle portrait of yourself in thick black glasses, an olive corduroy shirt, and a herringbone wool overcoat. This prompt creates a moody, editorial Vogue-style photo with Rembrandt lighting, earthy desaturated tones, and hyper-realistic 8K skin detail.

The Prompt 

Medium shot, high-angle portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image  , wearing thick black-rimmed glasses, an olive green corduroy shirt, and a heavy olive herringbone wool overcoat draped over my shoulders. Pose is hands clasped in front of face, looking directly into the lens with a subtle smirk. Shot on Hasselblad X2D 100C with a 100mm f/2.2 lens, aperture at f/4 for sharp facial detail and slight depth Lighting is Rembrandt style with a soft key light from the upper right, creating deep shadows on the left. Color grade is desaturated, earthy tones, cinematic and moody. Visible accessories include a large emerald ring and a silver square watch. Hyper-realistic, 8k resolution, uncropped, raw photo, global illumination, ray tracing, microscopic skin detail, editorial vogue style. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a reference photo of your face to your AI image generator. 2. Copy the full prompt and paste it into the tool. 3. Ensure the aspect ratio is set to 9:16 or Portrait. 4. Generate the image. If the facial likeness isn't perfect, try a different reference photo or adjust the image weight setting if available. 5. The prompt includes 'do according to my gender' so the AI should match your appearance from the reference image.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Rembrandt lighting and why is it used in this prompt?
    Rembrandt lighting is a classic portrait technique where one side of the face is lit and the other side has a small triangle of light on the cheek. It creates depth, drama, and a moody atmosphere. This prompt uses it to achieve a cinematic, editorial look similar to high-end fashion magazines.
  • How do I get the exact corduroy texture in my AI image?
    The prompt includes specific fabric descriptions like 'olive green corduroy' and 'heavy olive herringbone wool.' AI generators trained on fashion photography will recognize these terms and render the textures. For best results, use a high-quality model like Midjourney or Stable Diffusion that understands fabric details.
  • What does 'global illumination' and 'ray tracing' mean in an AI prompt?
    These terms borrowed from 3D rendering tell the AI to calculate light realistically. Global illumination means light bounces naturally between surfaces. Ray tracing means light paths are followed accurately. Including them signals the AI to create realistic shadows, highlights, and skin subsurface scattering for a hyper-realistic result.
