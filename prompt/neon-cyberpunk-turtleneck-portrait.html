Neon Cyberpunk Turtleneck Portrait Prompt

AI Image Prompts

Author: promptgalary

Create a cinematic low-angle portrait with your exact likeness wearing a turtleneck and retro sunglasses under vibrant magenta and cyan neon lights against a black background. Perfect for a modern cyberpunk aesthetic.

The Prompt 

A cinematic close-up low-angle portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image wearing a fitted ribbed turtleneck sweater and sleek retro-futuristic dark sunglasses, gazing slightly upward with a calm yet confident expression. The face should match the uploaded reference image accurately, preserving natural facial structure, skin tone, and identity without distortion. The lighting features vibrant neon tones with soft magenta and pink underlighting casting subtle shadows across the face and highlighting the texture of the ribbed sweater. A gentle cyan and blue rim light outlines the jawline and hair, separating the subject from a deep black background. The sunglasses reflect abstract neon geometric patterns in cyan and pink, adding a subtle cyberpunk touch without overpowering the portrait. Rendered in a clean, modern digital illustration style with realistic skin texture, detailed fabric definition, cinematic lighting, and smooth high-resolution finish. Sharp focus, balanced contrast, professional studio composition. do according to my gender . ar 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, front-facing photo of the person whose likeness you want to use. 2. Copy the entire prompt and paste it into your AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion). 3. If your tool supports parameters, include --ar 9:16 for the correct vertical aspect ratio. 4. Generate the image. If the face isn't a perfect match, try increasing the image weight (--iw in Midjourney) or run multiple variations.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'retro-futuristic dark sunglasses' look like?
    They are typically sleek, dark-lensed sunglasses with a design inspired by 80s sci-fi movies—often with angular or wraparound shapes and reflective lenses.
  • Can I use this for a female portrait?
    Yes, the 'do according to my gender' instruction tells the AI to adapt the clothing and style naturally to fit the person in your reference photo, whether male or female.
  • What is 'underlighting' in photography?
    Underlighting means the light source is positioned below the subject's face. It creates dramatic, often moody shadows and is a classic technique in cinematic and cyberpunk imagery.
