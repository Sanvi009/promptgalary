An AI image prompt that creates realistic stage speaker portraits with podium, microphones, and professional event lighting using your reference photo.

The Prompt 

A charismatic character with  exactly same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image   standing at a wooden podium giving a speech. i gesture with his right hand raised, palm open. i wearing exact same outfit from the image . Several microphones and lot's of different types of microphone are attached to the podium. The background is a blurred blue event screen or curtain. 1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will create a professional speaker portrait. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. The outfit from your reference photo will be used.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I change the hand gesture?
    Yes, replace 'right hand raised, palm open' with 'pointing finger', 'hands on podium', or 'holding notes' for different speaking poses.
  • What if my reference photo has casual clothes?
    The prompt uses 'exact same outfit from the image' - for professional results, use a business attire photo as reference.
  • How do I make it look more like a conference?
    Add 'large audience in blurred background' or 'conference logo on podium' to enhance the event atmosphere.
