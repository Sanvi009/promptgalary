Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'tall buildings on both sides creating a corridor' mean?
    The street is framed by vertical architecture on left and right, leading the eye toward the subject or deep into the background. Creates a tunnel-like perspective.
  • How ruined should the city look?
    Prompt says 'ruined city street' - expect cracked pavement, damaged building facades, debris, broken windows. Not pristine urban environment.
  • What is 'desaturated teal/green-gray color palette'?
    Muted, low-saturation colors with a cool tint. Inspired by dystopian films and melancholy cinematography. Skin tones remain natural but shifted cool.
