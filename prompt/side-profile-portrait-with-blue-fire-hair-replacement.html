How to Create a Side Profile Portrait with Realistic Blue Fire Hair

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-27
Author: promptgalary

Generate an ultra-realistic side profile portrait where your hair is replaced with realistic blue fire that illuminates your face, using cinematic lighting and shallow depth of field.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Use my photo as base. Keep my real face and proportions 100% unchanged. Side profile, head slightly lowered. Replace my hair with realistic blue fire following the natural hairline and head shape. Deep blue at the base fading to cyan at the tips, subtle transparency and light smoke. The flame acts as the main light source, creating soft blue rim lighting on my face edges Dark navy gradient background, cinematic lighting, natural skin texture (no smoothing), ultra-realistic, 85mm lens look, shallow depth of field, high detail, ar-9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Start by uploading a clear side profile photo of yourself to your AI image generator. The reference image is absolutely critical for this prompt because it demands that your face and proportions remain '100% unchanged.' Choose a photo with good lighting where your hairline is clearly visible, as the AI needs to understand where your natural hair begins and ends to replace it convincingly with flames. Copy the prompt exactly as written and paste it alongside your uploaded image. If your platform supports image prompts, make sure to select your photo as the primary reference before adding the text. Some tools work better when you provide the image URL first, then the text prompt. Generate multiple variations and examine each one carefully. The key elements to check are: does your face still look exactly like you, has the blue fire been applied only to the hair area following your natural hairline, and does the lighting on your face come from the flames as described. The flame should be the main light source, creating soft blue rim lighting along the edges of your face. If the fire doesn't blend naturally with your hairline, try using inpainting tools to refine the transition area. Some platforms allow you to select the hair region and regenerate just that part while keeping your face locked. Pay attention to the color fade from deep blue at the base to cyan at the tips, and look for the subtle smoke effect that adds realism.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I ensure my face stays exactly the same and doesn't change?
    Use the highest quality reference photo possible and make sure your platform supports strong image prompting. Some tools like Midjourney have a 'image weight' parameter that lets you prioritize the reference image over the text prompt.
  • What if the fire doesn't follow my natural hairline correctly?
    Try generating multiple variations or use inpainting to manually refine the hairline area. You can also add 'exactly following the hairline, no flames on forehead or skin' to reinforce the instruction.
  • Why specify 'no smoothing' in the prompt?
    AI generators sometimes automatically smooth skin, making it look plastic or airbrushed. 'No smoothing' tells the AI to preserve natural skin texture, pores, and subtle imperfections for a more realistic, photographic result.
