Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the AI to copy my face exactly?
    You must use the image upload feature in your AI tool (like Midjourney's /describe or Stable Diffusion's img2img). Upload a clear, front-facing photo before running the prompt.
  • What does 'South-Indian cinematic movie poster' style mean?
    It refers to the dramatic, high-contrast, often epic poster style from Indian Tamil, Telugu, or Malayalam films, known for intense portraits and dynamic action scenes.
  • Can I use this for a commercial project like a real movie?
    AI-generated images may have copyright restrictions. For a commercial movie poster, consult the terms of your AI generator and consider involving a graphic designer for final artwork.
