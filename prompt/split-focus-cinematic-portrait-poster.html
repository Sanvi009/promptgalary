Split Focus Cinematic Portrait Poster Prompt

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-17
Author: promptgalary

Generate a minimalist cinematic poster with your exact likeness split vertically: one side in sharp red/orange focus, the other dissolving into a blue halftone blur. Includes the word 'Focus' at the bottom for a modern editorial aesthetic.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

A cinematic portrait of me with the exact same facial features as the uploaded reference image — identical face structure, same eyes, nose, lips, skin tone, and expression. Preserve 100% likeness with no facial changes. i wearing a modern minimalist outfit: a dark navy oversized hoodie layered under a structured black jacket, clean collar line, subtle texture, contemporary street-style aesthetic. Outfit should look realistic, well-fitted, and softly lit by studio lighting. The composition is split vertically: • Right side of his face is sharply focused and illuminated with warm red/orange light. • Left side softly dissolves into a blue halftone blur blending into a deep blue gradient background. A subtle white camera focus frame overlay is placed over the sharp side of my face. Minimalist poster design with the word "Focus" centered near the bottom. High contrast cinematic lighting, ultra-sharp skin detail on focused side, soft diffusion on blurred side, clean composition, editorial poster aesthetic, 9:16 aspect ratio, high resolution. do according to my gender

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, front-facing reference photo of the person you want to feature. 2. Copy the full prompt into your AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion). 3. Use the --ar 9:16 parameter for the correct vertical poster format. 4. Generate the image. You may need to run several variations to get the split effect and text placement exactly as desired.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a 'camera focus frame overlay'?
    It's a graphic element that looks like the rectangular focus points you see in a camera viewfinder or on a smartphone screen. It adds a subtle, modern photography-themed detail to the design.
  • How does the split composition work here?
    The image is divided vertically down the center. The right side is in sharp focus with warm red/orange light, representing clarity. The left side is intentionally blurred with a cool blue halftone effect, representing abstraction or motion.
  • What style of clothing is 'dark navy oversized hoodie layered under a structured black jacket'?
    This is a popular contemporary street-style look. The oversized hoodie adds a casual, comfortable layer, while the structured jacket on top provides shape and a more polished, fashion-forward silhouette.
