Street Art Mixed Media Portrait Sunglasses Ink Collage AI Prompt

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-12
Author: promptgalary

Generate a gritty street-art editorial portrait from your photo. Black-and-white ink sketch with cross-hatching, translucent black sunglasses, vintage newspaper collage background, orange and blue paint splashes. 9:16.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Using my uploaded image as the primary subject reference, recreate a high-impact mixed-media editorial portrait in a gritty street-art illustration style. The subject is shown from the chest up, head tilted slightly upward, wearing bold translucent black sunglasses that act as the main color accent. Render the face and body in detailed black-and-white ink sketching with rough pencil lines, cross-hatching, and hand-drawn texture. The background should be a layered collage of vintage newspaper clippings and typography, partially visible and distressed, blended with expressive paint splashes in vibrant orange and deep blue scattered dynamically across the canvas. Add ink splatters, paint drips, grain, and paper texture for a raw, rebellious, contemporary poster feel. High contrast, dramatic lighting, sharp detail on facial features, energetic composition, urban art aesthetic, modern editorial illustration, no realism, no smooth skin, no photorealistic finish. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo as an image prompt. 2. Copy the full prompt exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use moderate image weight to preserve pose and sunglasses, but allow style transfer. 6. Generate multiple times to achieve balanced collage and paint splash composition.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'translucent black sunglasses' look like?
    Semi-transparent dark lenses where you can partially see the eyes beneath. Not opaque. Acts as a color accent despite being black due to sheen and reflection.
  • Will the AI include real newspaper text?
    Vintage newspaper clippings will show blurred, fragmented, or distressed lettering, not legible articles. The prompt says 'partially visible and distressed'.
  • How is this different from photorealistic portraits?
    No smooth skin, no soft gradients, no photographic lighting. Uses visible linework, cross-hatching, rough pencil texture, and paint splatters. Looks handmade.
