How to Generate an Ultra-Cinematic Freefall Portrait Above Earth

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-28
Author: promptgalary

Create a stunning 360° action-cam view of a person freefalling high above Earth with curved horizon, city below, and detailed streetwear fashion. Perfect for AI image generators.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Ultra-cinematic 360° action-cam view high above Earth, curved horizon and city far below. A person (use uploaded face) captured in a realistic freefall pose: body slightly arched belly down, arms naturally spread for balance, fingers relaxed (no stiff gestures), knees slightly bent, feet trailing behind with red white Jordan sneakers foreshortened in foreground. White hoodie with FOLLOW graffiti text on front, red bomber, red cargo pants whipping in wind, focused confident smile.

How to Use This Prompt

Start by uploading a clear front-facing photo of the person you want to feature in this freefall scene. The AI needs a good reference to match facial features accurately, so choose an image with even lighting and minimal shadows. Once uploaded, paste the entire prompt into your preferred AI image generator like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion. This prompt creates a complex perspective shot, so the first generation might not get everything perfect. Pay special attention to the 360° action-cam view—this tells the AI to create a wide, immersive perspective that shows the Earth's curvature. If the horizon looks flat or the city below is unclear, try adding terms like "ultra-wide angle lens" or "fisheye effect" to strengthen the perspective. The body position requires specific attention: "slightly arched belly down, arms naturally spread for balance, fingers relaxed." These details prevent the common AI mistake of generating stiff, mannequin-like poses. If the pose looks unnatural, you can regenerate or use inpainting tools to adjust specific body parts. The Jordan sneakers should appear in the foreground with foreshortening—this means they'll look larger and slightly distorted due to the camera angle, which adds to the realistic action-cam feel. Clothing details matter for the overall aesthetic. The white hoodie with "FOLLOW" graffiti text, red bomber jacket, and red cargo pants all need to show wind movement. If the fabric looks static, add terms like "fabric rippling in wind" or "dynamic wind effect" to enhance the sense of motion. The focused confident smile on the subject's face should look natural, not forced—if expressions appear odd, regenerate with "natural smile" or "relaxed expression" added. For best results with the 360° view, consider using a 16:9 aspect ratio or even a panoramic format to capture the full sweep of Earth below. You can also experiment with adding atmospheric details like clouds, sunlight reflecting off the city, or a gradient from blue sky to dark space at the top of the frame.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does my generated image not show the Earth's curve clearly?
    If the Earth's curve isn't prominent, try adding 'high altitude' or 'edge of space' to the prompt. You can also specify 'visible atmospheric blue halo' or 'thin blue line of atmosphere' to emphasize the curvature more dramatically.
  • How can I make the Jordan sneakers look more realistic and detailed?
    Add specific details like 'authentic Nike Air Jordan logo,' 'visible stitching,' and 'reflective red leather' to the prompt. You can also mention 'detailed shoe texture' or 'hyperrealistic sneakers' for better results.
  • The 'FOLLOW' text on the hoodie keeps coming out wrong. How do I fix this?
    Keep the text in quotation marks and consider adding 'bold graffiti font' or 'clear street art lettering.' Some AI generators handle text better if you place it earlier in the prompt or use a platform specifically trained on typography.
