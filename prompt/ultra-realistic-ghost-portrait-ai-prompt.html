Ultra Realistic Ghost Portrait AI Prompt

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-01-31
Author: promptgalary

This AI prompt creates a dramatic, cinematic black and white portrait where your likeness is haunted by a semi-transparent shadow entity made of swirling smoke with glowing eyes.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Create a 8k portrait ultra-realistic dark, cinematic ghost fantasy scene photography of me exact same facial and pysical features from uploaded image with same hair style. me standing confidently one side pose with looking slightly at the camera. Add a semi-transparent shadow entity made of swirling black smoke with glowing white eyes emerging from behind. Let the smoke wrap naturally around the body and blend into the floor. Use a black-and-white ghost-themed background, high contrast, moody cinematic lighting, ultra-realistic. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the full prompt text. 3. For best results, adjust the prompt to specify your desired gender (e.g., 'man' or 'woman') if not implied. 4. Use the 9:16 aspect ratio for a vertical portrait. 5. Generate the image and refine with variations if needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I use my own face in this AI ghost portrait?
    Most AI image generators have an 'upload image' feature. Use a clear, well-lit frontal photo of yourself as a reference image, then use this prompt.
  • Which AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    This prompt works well in Midjourney for artistic style, DALL-E 3 for prompt understanding, and Stable Diffusion with proper checkpoints for photorealism.
  • Can I get a color version of this ghost portrait?
    Yes. Simply remove the 'black-and-white' phrase from the prompt or change it to 'desaturated colors' or 'cool color palette'.
