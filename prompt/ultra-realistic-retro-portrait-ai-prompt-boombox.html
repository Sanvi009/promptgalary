Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I ensure the AI preserves my exact facial features?
    Use a high-quality reference photo and an AI tool with strong image reference capabilities. The prompt includes multiple preservation instructions: 'exact same facial and physical identity,' 'preserve original skull geometry,' 'identical jawline and stubble,' and multiple 'no change' commands. This redundancy helps guide the AI.
  • What does 'no age shift' mean in this prompt?
    It instructs the AI not to make you look older or younger than in your reference photo. Some AI generators tend to smooth skin or change perceived age. This term prevents that, ensuring your exact age appearance is maintained.
  • Why specify 'skull geometry' and 'bone structure'?
    These anatomical terms give the AI specific aspects of your face to preserve beyond just surface features. They help maintain the underlying structure that makes your face uniquely yours, preventing the AI from altering proportions while adding the retro styling.
