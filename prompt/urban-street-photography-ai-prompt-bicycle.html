Urban Street Photography AI Prompt with Bicycle

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-02-14
Author: promptgalary

Generate a realistic top-down aerial street photo of a person with your exact likeness standing on a road crossing next to a black bicycle. This prompt creates an editorial fashion image with a minimalist style, geometric road patterns, and moody urban color grading.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Top-down aerial street photography of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image standing on an urban road crossing, holding a black bicycle beside him. He wears a white oversized t-shirt, loose blue jeans, white sneakers, looking up confidently toward the camera. Minimalist street fashion vibe. Road markings with bold white lines and textured asphalt create strong geometric patterns, teal bike lane contrast, cinematic composition. Moody urban color grading, sharp focus, high detail, natural daylight, editorial fashion photography style, ultra-realistic, 4K resolution, clean shadows, modern street aesthetic. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear photo of your face/full body to your AI image generator (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL·E 3). 2. Copy and paste the full prompt into the tool. 3. If the tool supports it, set the aspect ratio to 9:16 or Portrait. 4. Generate the image. If the face isn't perfect, try using a different reference image or adjust the 'image weight' setting if available.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    This prompt is optimized for advanced generators like Midjourney and Stable Diffusion because they handle specific camera angles, clothing details, and color grading very well. DALL·E 3 can also produce good results, especially with the ultra-realistic style.
  • How do I get my exact face in the generated image?
    You need to use a tool that supports face swapping or reference image features. In Midjourney, you would upload your photo and use its URL in the prompt. In Stable Diffusion, you might use an extension like Roop or Reactor. The prompt includes 'exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image' to instruct the AI to prioritize this.
  • What does 'aspect ratio 9:16' mean?
    9:16 is a portrait-oriented aspect ratio, perfect for images designed for mobile phones, social media stories (like Instagram or TikTok), or vertical posters. It's the opposite of the standard landscape 16:9 ratio.
